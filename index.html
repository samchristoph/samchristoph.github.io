About Me

Hi there, my name is Sam!

I am currently a Junior at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, majoring in Systems Engineering and Design with a minor in Computer Science.

I've been interested in the way computers function for as long as I can remember, pursuing everything from early Unity game development, to tinkering with Arduino components, to sorting thousands of rows of data entries using Python. My interest in programming has led me to pursue a minor in Computer Science, and I continue to grow as both a programmer and an engineer through my recent coursework and personal projects.

I understand the importance of self learning, and will generally make it my mission to understand the big picture as well as the minute details of any given obstacle.
